Reminiscing

01 Oct, 2023

Visiting all those places where we dreamed you’d be,
Not feeling alone, but there’s an absence in me.
I’ve made strides since then, and I hope you have too,
Life’s path now clearer, confidence anew.

Should fate once again intertwine our two lives,
Let’s seize the chance, like old friends who survive.
A toast to the memories, the laughter and tears,
In each story, we’ll find echoes of our yesteryears.

Composition: Kamefrede
