Hello Internet

20 Nov, 2020

After much delaying, I finally caved in and set up a website for real. This is my 3rd attempt at a website, I think.

The first attempt went badly since I was trying to minimize the use of Javascript but I still wanted to have fancy animations and navbars and whatnot.

For my second attempt I realized that I didn’t need any of that fanciness and was recommended by my friend Williewillus to check out both Jekyll and Hugo.

That attempt quickly ended because COVID-19 struck and I forgot about it. Oh well…

And here we are now, hopefully this time for good! A really simple website hosted on Github Pages, because I have no need for something fancier than that.
