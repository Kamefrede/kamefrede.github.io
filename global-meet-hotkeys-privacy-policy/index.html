Privacy Policy for Global Google Meet Hotkeys

Effective Date: March 4, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Global Google Meet Hotkeys Chrome Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding this data.

Data Collection and Usage

The Global Google Meet Hotkeys extension is designed to provide global hotkeys for controlling Google Meet sessions. To achieve this, the extension collects and uses the following data:

  1. Google Meet Tab ID
    • What is collected: The unique ID of the Google Meet tab you are using.
    • Why it is collected: To identify the correct tab for performing actions such as toggling the microphone, camera, or focusing on the Meet session.
    • How it is stored: The tab ID is temporarily stored in the browser’s local storage. This data is cleared when the browser is closed or the Meet session ends.
  2. Google Meet Tab URL
    • What is collected: The unique URL of the Google Meet tab you are using.
    • Why it is collected: To allow the user to quickly open a bookmarked Google Meet tab with a single hotkey.
    • How it is stored: The tab URL is temporarily stored in the browser’s local storage. This data is cleared when a user clears their browsing data.

No other data is collected, stored, or shared.

Data Sharing

The extension does not share, transmit, or sell any data to third parties.

Permissions

This extension requests the following permissions:

  • Access to https://meet.google.com/*: To interact with your Google Meet session for muting/unmuting the microphone, toggling the camera, and focusing on the Meet tab.
  • Storage: To store the tab ID temporarily to ensure the extension functions correctly.
  • Tabs: To store the tab URL and to be able to open a new tab with the stored tab URL.

These permissions are used exclusively for the stated purposes and nothing else.

Your Rights

As this extension does not collect any personal or identifiable information, there are no rights to request, delete, or amend data related to personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the extension, feel free to reach out at kamefrede@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, with the updated effective date clearly stated.
